About

A New Mexico builder, end to end.

Straight A Builders is a family-run residential construction company serving northern and central New Mexico. We design and build homes that respect the land, the climate, and the way our clients actually live.

From foundation to finish carpentry, every project is led by a senior builder on site — no handoffs, no surprises. Just straight lines, square corners, and houses that hold up.

  • Licensed & insured general contractor
  • In-house framing, finish, and project management
  • Trusted local subcontractor network
  • Transparent budgets and fixed-bid options
